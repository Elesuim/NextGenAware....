Mission

This website addresses the critical social issue of teenage pregnancy in the Philippines, aiming to empower adolescents with knowledge and resources for informed decisions about their reproductive and sexual health. Recognizing the severe health, social, and economic consequences highlighted by statistics like the 2021 YAFS5 study, which revealed 6.8% of Filipino girls aged 15-19 had begun childbearing, this platform seeks to raise awareness and promote effective prevention strategies. By providing accurate information on the physical, emotional, psychological, and social effects of teenage pregnancy, alongside guidance on prevention methods and safe sexual practices, the website aims to reduce teenage pregnancy rates and support a healthier future for Filipino youth.
